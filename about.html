About Us

👋

Welcome!

Welcome to AI Sign Language Learning, a platform where curiosity meets creativity. This website was created by Shrida Prathap, a high school student passionate about languages and inclusive communication. Shrida wanted to make learning sign language interactive, accessible, and fun for everyone.

🎓

Structured Courses

Our courses guide learners step by step, breaking down gestures, hand movements, and facial expressions. Each lesson teaches how to use signs meaningfully, allowing users to progress at their own pace while building confidence.

🤖

AI-Powered Practice

Practice with our AI assistant! Receive instant feedback, refine your gestures, and retain what you’ve learned. The AI acts as your personal tutor, making learning fun and effective.

📝

Tests and Progress

Track your learning with quizzes and assessments. Identify strengths, work on weak areas, and unlock badges as you improve. Progress bars and scores motivate you every step of the way.

📹

Video Library

Watch sign language in real-life contexts. From casual conversations to storytelling, our videos help you understand gestures, expressions, and communication flow, giving a deeper understanding of the language.

🌏

Why Learn Sign Language?

Learning sign language opens doors to inclusive communication, cognitive growth, and cultural understanding. Explore, practice, test yourself, and immerse in videos. This platform empowers everyone to communicate beyond words.
